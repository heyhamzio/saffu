HomeGlazer started with one clear user problem: people hesitate to purchase paint because they cannot imagine the final wall result.

The solution strategy:

- Build a guided color preview experience.
- Keep interaction fast on mobile.
- Make confidence visible with side-by-side variations.
- Reduce decision fatigue with curated combinations.

From a product point of view, the win is not visual delight alone. The win is reducing uncertainty before checkout.
