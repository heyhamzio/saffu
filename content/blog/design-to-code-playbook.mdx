Good design is not finished in Figma. It is finished when users can click, trust, and complete the job.

This is the simple workflow I use:

1. Define user outcome first, screens second.
2. Turn key flows into reusable component decisions.
3. Pair design tokens with engineering constraints early.
4. Ship the smallest complete loop.
5. Add polish after behavior is validated.

The goal is to reduce design-dev friction and improve launch speed without dropping quality.
